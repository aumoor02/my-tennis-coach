Pricing Model

Get access to our Community and Equipment Sections.

With our free tier, customers will communicate with tennis enthusiasts from all over the world. Tennis players and coaches will have access to equipment specs and reviews, a growing community of like-minded individuals where they can discuss tennis topics, and access to our local and regional leagues!

Get access to all of our MTC resources.

Tennis athletes and coaches will not only have access to our Community and Equipment sections, but they will also gain access to our Resources section. Here, tennis coaches and athletes will learn the fundamental aspects of the various tennis strokes, progressions, and a variety of drills to challenge players to grow.

Get access to all of our MTC resources year-round.

By enrolling in our Tier 3 program, players will obtain all the same resources as Tier 2, but at a discount.

Get access to all of our MTC resources year-round. Plus, have access to our online coaches.

Tennis athletes can obtain our Ultimate Package by enrolling in this tier. Athletes can send in videos of their strokes, and the tennis coaches here at My Tennis Coach will perform a video analysis. Our coaches will then pinpoint the flaws in your technique and give you step-by-step guidance on how to fix those flaws.
